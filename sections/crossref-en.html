FRBRoo's 'Expression Fragment' is by definition a fragment of an expression, which is the immaterial realisation of a work - and for this reason cannot be object of a citation or a detailed bibliographical record (an expression has no page numbers). The FRBRoo 'Publication Expression' is itself an immaterial (ideal) version of a text.
