Collection of bibliographic information

Our choice of literary texts was: Prometheus Bound and Trojan Women by Aeschylus, Agamemnon and Medea by Euripides, based on the criteria of linguistic and content variety and presence in our bibliographic sources. We asked ourselves: In which moments and contexts men turn to divine entities in Greek tragedies? / In which forms do they do it? / What kind of divine interventions is it possible to classify? / Is man impotent when he has to face divine entities? / In which cases human will and actions coincide with those of divine entities? / In which cases is it possible to recognize a similarity between a man and a divine entity?
